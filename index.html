Welcome to Creative Game Design

We're going to teach you to innovate in a way you've never seen

What you're going to learn

In CGD, you are not going to only be making games. More often than not, you'll be learning how to make cool stuff. We are going to teach you to use and master game engines, which is what big companies use in order to create big titles such as Battlefield, League of Legends, Call of Duty. You'll experience VR, AR, and sensor-driven game creation. You'll control virtual drones with nothing but your hand. Every single game has a ton of code and creativity behind it and that's what CGD is offering:

A chance at the whole experience of designing a game.


Imagine. Create. Code.
