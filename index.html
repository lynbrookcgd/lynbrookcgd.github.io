Creative Game Design (CGD) is an aspiring Lynbrook club created to teach and explore the unique field of game creation. Take a look.

⇩

Lynbrook Creative Game Design will catapult your ability to code and design video games. Interested in listening to talks from professional game designers, participating in national competitions, improving your programming skills, and having fun while you're at it? You're at the right place.

Curriculum

In our club, you will be taught to use a a variety of game development tools, from game engines to game objects. You will also be learning the programming skills necessary to sucessfully use these tools, including C#, JavaScript, and much more! Want to improve your coding skills? Our curriculum teaches you both the software and physical aspects of game design: from ideas, to design, to coding

➜

➜

Activities

We plan on hosting a variety of events, from hackathons to professional talks, to further your understanding of game design to a higher level. In hackathons, members will be able to collaborate on a project idea in teams and collectively create something to compete with other such teams. The club will also be inviting high-level, experienced game developers to give talks and lectures at our school, in hopes of creating a unique experience for members, as well as showing the real-life applications of the skills you will learn in our club.
